/********
Bitwise :
********/

Operate on bits of int, long, byte or short.

~ bitwise unary not
& bitwise AND
| bitwise OR
^ bitwise XOR
>> right shift (preserves higher order bit!)
<< left shift (brings zero)
>>> unsigned shift (brings zero)
